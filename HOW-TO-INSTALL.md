# How to Install My Board
## On your iPhone (takes 30 seconds)
1. Open **Safari** on your iPhone
2. Go to this link: [YOUR LINK HERE — your mum will send it to you]
3. Tap the **Share button** at the bottom of the screen (the box with an arrow pointing up ↑)
4. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Tap **"Add"**
6. Done! The app icon appears on your home screen — tap it anytime to open

## On your Mac (takes 30 seconds)
1. Open **Chrome** on your Mac
2. Go to the same link your mum sent you
3. Look for a small **⊕ icon** on the right side of the address bar
4. Click it and click **"Install"**
5. Done! The app appears in your Applications folder and dock

## That's it!
Your tasks sync automatically between your phone and Mac.
No account needed. No password. Just open and go.
